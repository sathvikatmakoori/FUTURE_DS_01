| Unique Customers | **3,844** |

### Top Insights
1. **Technology dominates** — 52.9% of revenue ($1.34M), led by Laptop Pro 15" at $690K
2. **Q4 holiday surge** — Nov–Dec generate $600K, a 68% spike vs monthly average
3. **Online channel leads** — 44.1% of orders and $1.12M revenue
4. **SMB segment** — Highest revenue segment at $805K, strong repeat buyers
5. **East region leads** — $661K, but South underperforms by 10% — an opportunity
