Data-Driven Insights
📦 Product Strategy
Laptop Pro 15" is the single biggest revenue driver
At $690K revenue (27.3% of total), it outperforms the next product by 2×. High average unit price ($1,059) combined with strong repeat demand makes it the cornerstone product. Consider premium support bundles or accessories cross-sell.
📅 Seasonal Pattern
Q4 holiday surge delivers 23.9% of annual revenue
November–December alone generate $600K — a 68% spike vs. the monthly average. The business should front-load inventory by September and run targeted campaigns in October to capture early shoppers and maximize the seasonal window.
🌍 Geographic Opportunity
East region leads, but South has untapped potential
East ($661K) edges West ($638K) by only 3.6%, suggesting competitive parity. South trails by 10% despite similar population coverage. Targeted regional campaigns or local partnerships in Southern cities could close this gap.
🛒 Channel Insight
Online channel is the primary growth engine
Online accounts for 44.1% of orders and $1.12M revenue — outperforming In-Store by 40%. However, In-Store has higher average order values, suggesting it attracts more deliberate, high-value purchases. Both deserve investment but with different tactics.
🧩 Category Mix
Technology dominates but Office Supplies has hidden value
Technology (52.9% revenue, 35% of orders) has high revenue per order. Office Supplies has 1,493 orders but only $220K revenue — indicating low unit prices but high frequency. A subscription or bundling model could significantly grow that segment's LTV.
👥 Customer Segments
SMB segment is the silent winner — invest here
SMBs generate $805K (31.8%) with 1,696 orders, edging out Consumers despite lower order count. Their consistent ordering pattern suggests strong retention. Loyalty programs, account managers, or B2B pricing tiers could significantly expand this segment.
Strategic Recommendations
01
Double Down on Technology — Especially Laptops
Technology generates 52.9% of revenue with the highest margins. Prioritize stocking Laptop Pro 15" and Monitor 27" year-round. Launch a "Work From Home Bundle" pairing both with a Mechanical Keyboard at a slight discount — likely to drive 15–20% upsell conversion.
📈 Estimated Impact: +$120K–180K annual revenue
02
Activate a Q4 Pre-Campaign Starting in October
The business naturally peaks in Nov–Dec. Getting ahead with email campaigns, early-bird promotions, and inventory pre-positioning in October can capture the 10–15% of buyers who shop early. Offer a "Black Friday Preview" in late October for email subscribers.
📈 Estimated Impact: +$40K–60K in Q4 uplift
03
Launch a SMB Loyalty & Volume Program
With 1,696 SMB orders averaging $475/order, a tiered loyalty program (Silver/Gold/Platinum) offering 5–12% discounts at volume thresholds would incentivize consolidation of purchases. A dedicated account rep for SMBs ordering $5K+/year could double retention rates.
📈 Estimated Impact: +$95K from improved retention
04
Invest in the South Region with Targeted Marketing
The South trails peers by $67K–68K in revenue despite similar order counts (1,258 vs avg 1,250). This points to lower average order values, not fewer customers. A targeted campaign highlighting high-ticket items (Standing Desk, Laptop Pro) could lift AOV by 15%.
📈 Estimated Impact: +$55K–80K in regional revenue
